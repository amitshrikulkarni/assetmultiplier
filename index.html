Investment Philosophy

Asset Multiplier is an equity research service founded by Prashant Vaishampayan - who is registered with SEBI as a Research Analyst. Our aim is to pick Long Term Multi-bagger stocks by following a tried and tested Value Investment Process backed by our decades of experience. The Value Investment Process

Identifying Long Term Multi Baggers

Based on the Value Investment Process, we provide one Stock Idea every month with a long term view of investment. Typical Holding horizon of the stock should be 1 to 3 years. To provide you the Market Outlook and to take care of your other investments, we would also send one Macro-economic Report every month.

History of Solid Results

We diligently follow principles of Benjamin Graham and have been rigorously evaluating stocks across sectors since 2011. This has enabled us to identify companies like PI Industries, Aarti Industries, IPCA, Torrent Pharma, Cadilla, Vinati Organics which have become multi bagger in the last four years.

Our IRR over this period is near 30%.
